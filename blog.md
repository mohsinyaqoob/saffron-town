## INTERNAL LINKING RULES (apply to every post)
1. Link to /shop/saffron at least twice per post — once mid-article, once at the end CTA.
2. Link to /lab-reports when mentioning "purity", "ISO 3632", "lab-tested", or "certified".
3. Link to /reviews when mentioning "trusted by families" or "thousands of customers".
4. Every post must link to at least 3 other blog posts using the exact anchor keyword.
5. Never use generic anchor text like "click here" — always use the keyword phrase as the anchor.
6. FAQ section at the end must include at least one question that links to a related blog.
